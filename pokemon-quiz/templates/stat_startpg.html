Multiplier Quiz

The goal of this quiz is to guess which Pokémon has the highest stat.

Rules

You will be shown four Pokémon and a question in the middle asking you which of the four has the highest of a given stat. If two or more Pokémon share the highest value, they will all be correct.

Hints

  • A Pokémon has 6 different stats: hp, attack, defence, special attack (spatk), special defence (spedef), and speed, as well as "total" which is the sum of all its stats.
